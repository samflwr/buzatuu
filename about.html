biography

samir buzatu is a romanian ai software engineer and production designer working at the intersection of cinematic systems and visual storytelling. born on 10 january 2004 in baia de aramă, he began building ai-driven tooling early and evolved that practice into film-scale pipelines and visual design.

in 2021 he founded vivienne ai, a neural network initiative focused on processing complex datasets for space-oriented applications. his credits span production design, vfx supervision, and conceptual photography with a focus on clarity, continuity, and narrative impact.
